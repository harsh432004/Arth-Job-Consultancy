Internship

An internship is a temporary job or work experience program offered by an organization to students or recent graduates. It provides an opportunity for individuals to gain practical, hands-on experience in their field of study or interest. Interns work under the guidance and supervision of experienced professionals and are often involved in various aspects of the organization's operations. Internships can be paid or unpaid, full-time or part-time, and can last for a few weeks or several months.
